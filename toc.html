Linear Algebra @BUCS

This is a (draft) set of notes for CAS CS 132: Geometric Algorithms at Boston University. Admittedly, I was unsure if I wanted to take on this project (in a sense, I still am). I'm not convinced there's a need for another collection of notes on linear algebra. But it turns out I can't help myself. I am of the opinion that any good course needs to be personal, so these are my personal notes, which follow my way of thinking about and organizing the material.
